How to use:

  • Wake-up.
  • Login.
  • Click the check-in button. (It actually checks you in as soon as you login, but you can press the button to be double-sure.)
  • Verify that it accepted your check-in. (It pulls the list of check-ins every 10 seconds.)
  • If there's an issue, send a text to the WhatsApp for now and let me know about it.

Warning: The login tokens only last for an hour, so you have to login every morning.
